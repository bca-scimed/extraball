Games (and how to play them)

Below is an ever-increasing list of pinball games. They include quick tips for play (about 3 bullet points for the impatient player aka me) and a more in-depth strategy section when applicable. While not as important for older games, most new games will have periodic game code updates, which can completely alter game-play strategy. I will do my best to keep the strategy section up-to-date when it comes to code version.
